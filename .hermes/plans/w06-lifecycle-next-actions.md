## Assumptions & contingencies

- The stabilized W05 correction keeps `jobos.outreach-outcome.v1`; W06 consumes `summarizeOutreachOutcomes` only and never duplicates record/correction/cohort logic. If W05 bumps the schema, the Phase 6 gate requires one clean consumer cutover after stabilization, with no dual-version shim.
- “Manual reschedule survives recomputation” means the same stage/action retains its user due date across note events, attestation replay, receipt confirmation, scheduler scans, and store reopen; a real stage/action change intentionally supersedes it because stage correctness outranks the obsolete schedule.
- “One active action” means one open `application_next_action` per active application. General operational tasks and W05 outreach-thread tasks may coexist but cannot be selected or labeled as the application’s current lifecycle action.
- Global operational tasks remain nullable-profile records and are visible only through explicit `tasks ... --global` or the TUI failure card; they are excluded from every profile review. If migration finds a task whose profile cannot be derived, it remains global rather than being guessed, copied, or deleted.
- Employer follow-up is one W06 reminder tied to immutable application submission evidence, not an outreach sequence. Completion/sending semantics are not added; the action persists until a stage change or manual reschedule, avoiding ownership of W05 thread cadence.
