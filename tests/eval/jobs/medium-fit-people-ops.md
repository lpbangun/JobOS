Title: People Operations Program Manager
Company: ScaleWorks Talent
Location: Boston, MA / Hybrid

ScaleWorks Talent supports early-stage companies with recruiting operations, onboarding, manager enablement, and employee experience programs. The People Operations Program Manager will build repeatable hiring and onboarding workflows while partnering with founders and department leads.

Responsibilities:
- Design structured recruiting and onboarding processes for growing teams.
- Build dashboards for funnel health, time-to-fill, and candidate experience.
- Facilitate manager training and process retrospectives.
- Coordinate cross-functional people programs with executives and team leads.

Requirements:
- Experience in HR, recruiting, people operations, or talent programs.
- Strong project management and stakeholder communication skills.
- Comfort building lightweight systems and process documentation.
- Interest in early-stage startup environments.

Compensation: $100k-$130k base.
Work model: Hybrid in Boston.
