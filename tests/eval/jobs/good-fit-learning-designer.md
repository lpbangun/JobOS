Title: Learning Experience Designer
Company: Pathway Labs
Location: Remote

Pathway Labs creates career mobility programs for adult learners and frontline workers. The Learning Experience Designer will design project-based learning experiences, interview subject matter experts, and evaluate learner outcomes in partnership with product and customer success.

Responsibilities:
- Design learning journeys, practice activities, rubrics, and facilitator guides.
- Conduct learner and employer research to improve program relevance.
- Partner with product managers to embed learning experiences into a digital platform.
- Use completion, activation, and learner feedback data to improve programs.

Requirements:
- Experience in learning design, curriculum development, EdTech, or workforce training.
- Strong writing, research, and stakeholder facilitation skills.
- Comfort connecting qualitative insight with outcome data.
- Interest in economic mobility and career education.

Compensation: $95k-$125k base.
Work model: Remote.
