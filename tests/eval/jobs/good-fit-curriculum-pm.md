Title: Curriculum Product Manager
Company: BrightLearn
Location: Remote US

BrightLearn is a learning platform used by school districts and workforce training providers. We are hiring a Curriculum Product Manager to lead educator discovery, translate instructional workflows into product requirements, and partner with design and engineering on AI-assisted curriculum planning tools.

Responsibilities:
- Lead user research with educators, curriculum specialists, and operations teams.
- Define product requirements for learning workflows, assessment authoring, and classroom implementation.
- Use activation and adoption data to prioritize roadmap decisions.
- Partner cross-functionally with engineering, design, support, and school success teams.

Requirements:
- 3+ years in product management, learning design, EdTech, or education technology operations.
- Experience turning qualitative research into product strategy.
- Strong communication with educators and non-technical stakeholders.
- Comfort using analytics dashboards to evaluate product adoption.

Compensation: $130k-$160k base plus benefits.
Work model: Remote within the United States.
