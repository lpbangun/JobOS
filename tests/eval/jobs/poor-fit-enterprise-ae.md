Location: Dallas, TX / Hybrid

- 6+ years enterprise SaaS sales experience with a record of exceeding quota.
- Experience negotiating six-figure software contracts.
- Comfort with high-volume outbound prospecting and travel.
- Based near Dallas or willing to relocate.

Compensation: $120k base / $240k OTE.
Work model: Hybrid with travel.
