# Product Manager, Learning Platform
Company: Example Learning Co
Location: Remote US

Example Learning Co builds tools for educators and workforce teams. We are looking for a Product Manager to lead discovery, roadmap planning, and launch execution for AI-assisted learning workflows.

Responsibilities:
- Lead customer discovery with educators, learners, and enterprise stakeholders.
- Define product strategy, metrics, and roadmap for an early-stage EdTech product.
- Partner with engineering and design to ship usable software in weekly increments.
- Analyze adoption data and improve activation, retention, and learning outcomes.

Requirements:
- 4+ years product management, education technology, learning design, or related operator experience.
- Strong written communication and stakeholder management.
- Evidence of launching products or programs with measurable outcomes.
- Comfort with remote collaboration across US time zones.

Benefits include remote work, health coverage, and salary range $130k-$165k.
